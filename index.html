The Library (which others call the Universe)

is a community building organization for readers, listeners, watchers

We want to build the largest library on Earth. It will be never burned or destroyed. Never more! Because it will be your library. The largest library on earth is at our homes. We want to connect them. We want to connect you.

We are working hard for the paper work and coding the actual web application to get it ready as soon as possible. But we also couldn’t wait any more to share this dream with you.

How you can be part of this then?

  • Send us an email through our contact form. We will add you to our whatsapp list and group.
  • Open a zotero account and download the software and it’s browser extension (Available for both chrome and firefox)
  • Take photos of your books and send it to us. We will help you set up your library in the Zotero Group and the rest. Add your books to our Zotero group.
